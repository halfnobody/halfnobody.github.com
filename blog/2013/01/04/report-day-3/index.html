Report: Day 3

Jan 4th, 2013 @ 12:13 AM

Today was an OK day. I got to work a little late, but I immediately completed my Project01 task in about ten minutes. I then wasted a few hours before having a quality afternoon working on Project02. I probably had four hours of effective work in about seven hours at the office. I was hoping to find some productivity software that would allow me to clock in and clock out and formalize the accounting of my time. I never got around to it, so I will have to do that tomorrow.

I also looked on Craigslist and applied for other “gigs” in town. I’m hoping to pick up some more temporary work that will enable me to make a few dollars and gain some experience with different types of projects. At the very least, I’ll get better at applying for jobs and meeting new people, which is something I should be better at than I am.

This evening I played basketball for a couple hours which was nice. My current weight makes it quite hard on my legs and knees, but I have to start somewhere. It was a bit rough because I still have a bit of a cold, but I was able to get up and down the court OK.
